Christopher Evans

Born in Harlem and raised in the South Bronx, Christopher Evans began his journey in the world of acting with a foundation built at Sledge Studios in NYC in 2008, later expanding his expertise with formal training at the Digital Film Academy in 2016.

His on-screen career began with background roles, including a turn as a court reporter on the original Law & Order in 2008, marking the first step in a path filled with diverse characters and stories.

Since late summer 2024, Christopher has starred in five student films from prestigious institutions such as NYU, Ghetto Film Academy, and CUNY, showcasing his versatility in improvisation, drama, horror, romance, and action across both screen and stage.

With a natural range and an unwavering commitment to his craft, Christopher continues to evolve as an actor, delivering performances that resonate with audiences worldwide.
